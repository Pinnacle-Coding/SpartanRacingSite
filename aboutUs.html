WHO WE ARE

  • AND WHY WE RACE

In order to compete in Formula SAE, the Spartan Racing team designs, builds, and tests a new Formula style racecar each year based on regulations and standards similar to those imposed on professional motor sport teamshis exercise immerses students of all backgrounds in an environment that parallels that of a fast-paced engineering work environment and professional race team. The multidimensional nature of the Formula SAE experience is an invaluable educational tool.
